# Our Story Section - Image Placeholders

## Required Images

Please add the following 3 images to this folder (`images/story/`):

### 1. `story-1.jpg`
- **Description**: Fresh seafood processing photo
- **Suggested content**: Workers processing fresh fish, cleaning/sorting seafood, or fish on processing table
- **Recommended size**: 400x500px (portrait orientation)
- **Position**: Main large image on the left

### 2. `story-2.jpg`
- **Description**: Quality control inspection photo
- **Suggested content**: Quality inspector checking fish, lab testing, or grading process
- **Recommended size**: 350x250px (landscape orientation)
- **Position**: Top right corner

### 3. `story-3.jpg`
- **Description**: Cold storage facility photo
- **Suggested content**: Cold storage room, frozen fish stacking, or refrigeration equipment
- **Recommended size**: 300x200px (landscape orientation)
- **Position**: Bottom right corner

## Image Tips
- Use high-quality photos from Oceanex facilities
- Ensure good lighting and professional appearance
- JPG format recommended for photos
- Compress images for web (under 200KB each ideally)
