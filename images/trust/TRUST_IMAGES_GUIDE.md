## üé® Design Tips

### Color Scheme:
- Primary: Navy Blue (#062351)
- Accent: Gold (#B8860B)

### Typography:
- Font: Poppins (same as website)
- Title: 600-700 weight, 32-48px
- Subtext: 400-500 weight, 14-18px
- All caps for titles

### Style:
- Clean and minimal
- Professional look
- High contrast for readability
- Center-aligned text
- Leave padding around edges (at least 40px)
