Our Journey

Building Trust Since 2018

From domestic distributor to international seafood exporter — a story of growth, partnership, and commitment to quality.

Company Established

PT Hijau Mitra Sejati was founded with a clear vision — connecting Indonesia's rich marine resources with domestic markets. Initial focus on local distribution and building strong relationships with fishing communities across Java.

Company Founded Domestic Market Focus

Export Operations Begin

Started export activities and began building brand positioning in international markets. Established key relationships with B2B buyers across Asia and the Middle East, laying the foundation for global expansion.
