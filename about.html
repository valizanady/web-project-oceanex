Our Story

Oceanex Marine Industries

Indonesia is home to some of the world's richest marine resources, spanning from the eastern waters of Papua to the western coasts of Sumatra. This abundance is both a strategic advantage and a responsibility.

At Oceanex Marine Industries, we believe our role extends beyond supplying quality seafood — we are committed to respecting and preserving the marine ecosystems that support our business and the livelihoods connected to them.

As beneficiaries of the ocean's bounty, we promote responsible sourcing and long-term stewardship to help ensure healthy fish populations for the future.
