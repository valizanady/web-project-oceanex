New Sustainability Certification Achieved for All Product Lines

Sarah Wijaya
Head of Communications

We are proud to announce that Oceanex Marine Industries has achieved the prestigious Marine Stewardship Council (MSC) certification across all our seafood product lines. This milestone represents our unwavering commitment to sustainable fishing practices and ocean conservation.

The certification process took over 18 months of rigorous auditing, documentation, and process improvements. Our team worked tirelessly with independent auditors to ensure every aspect of our supply chain meets the highest international standards for sustainability.

What This Certification Means

The MSC certification is recognized globally as the gold standard for sustainable seafood. It guarantees that our products come from fisheries that:

  • Maintain healthy fish populations and prevent overfishing
  • Minimize environmental impact on marine ecosystems
  • Implement effective fisheries management systems
  • Comply with relevant local and international laws
"This certification represents years of dedication to sustainable practices. We believe that protecting our oceans is not just good for the environment—it's essential for the long-term success of our industry and the communities that depend on it." — Budi Santoso, CEO of Oceanex Marine Industries

Our Sustainability Journey

This achievement is part of our broader sustainability strategy that we initiated in 2020. Over the past five years, we have:

  • Reduced our carbon footprint by 35% through energy-efficient cold storage
  • Eliminated single-use plastics from our packaging operations
  • Partnered with 50+ local fishing communities to promote sustainable practices
  • Invested $2 million in marine conservation programs

Our partnership with local fishermen ensures sustainable practices from catch to delivery.

What's Next

Looking ahead, we are committed to furthering our sustainability initiatives. In 2025, we plan to:

  • Achieve carbon-neutral operations at our main processing facility
  • Launch a blockchain-based traceability system for complete supply chain transparency
  • Expand our sustainable fisheries partnership program to cover 100+ communities
  • Obtain additional certifications including ASC (Aquaculture Stewardship Council)

A Message to Our Partners

We want to express our sincere gratitude to all our partners, suppliers, and customers who have supported us on this journey. Your commitment to sourcing sustainable seafood has made this achievement possible.

For those looking to partner with a sustainability-focused seafood supplier, we invite you to reach out to our team. Together, we can make a positive impact on our oceans while meeting the growing global demand for responsibly sourced seafood.
